# Privacy Policy for JobCruncher

**Last updated:** March 30, 2026

## Overview

JobCruncher is a browser extension that helps you save and track job applications from LinkedIn and Indeed. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the extension collects, how it is stored, and how it is used.

## Data Collection

JobCruncher collects the following information from job listing pages you visit on LinkedIn and Indeed:

- Job title
- Company name
- Location
- Salary (if listed)
- Number of applicants (if listed)
- Education and experience level (if listed)
- Work type (e.g., remote, hybrid, on-site)
- Featured benefits (if listed)
- Hiring contact name and profile URL (if listed)
- Job listing URL
- Date the job was saved

This data is collected **only when you explicitly save a job** (by clicking the "Save Job" button) or when you submit a job application on a supported site with auto-save enabled.

## Data Storage

All data is stored **locally on your device** using your browser's built-in storage (`chrome.storage.local`). Your data:

- Never leaves your browser
- Is never transmitted to any external server
- Is never shared with any third party
- Is never used for analytics, advertising, or tracking
- Persists across browser sessions until you choose to delete it

## Data Export

You may export your saved jobs as a CSV file at any time. Exported files are saved directly to your device. The "Export to Google Sheets" feature downloads a CSV file and opens the Google Sheets import page in a new tab. No data is sent to Google by the extension — you choose whether to import the file yourself.

## Permissions

The extension requests the following browser permissions:

- **storage** — To save your job data locally across browser sessions
- **activeTab** — To read job details from the page you are currently viewing
- **scripting** — To inject the save button and sidebar into job listing pages
- **tabs** — To open the Google Sheets import page when exporting

## Data Deletion

You can delete all saved data at any time by clicking the "Clear" button in the extension popup. Uninstalling the extension also removes all stored data.

## Third-Party Services

JobCruncher does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services. The extension operates entirely within your browser.
